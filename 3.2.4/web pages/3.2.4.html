2.4 I can describe the features, benefits and limitations of different collaborative technology tools and devices

The benefits of collaborative technology tools are that work gets done faster and can be done from anywhere and can be done from several devices that support the collaborative technologies

The limitations of them can be that the internet connection could be bad and the technology that one person uses could have some worse qualities like a slow machine or the machine could need updates
